Our Mission

Making Carbon Awareness Simple & Beautiful

We built GreenWallet because we believe awareness is the first step toward change. Most people don't know their carbon footprint — not because they don't care, but because the tools are complicated or boring.

We changed that. GreenWallet turns your daily habits into clear numbers, beautiful charts, and actionable tips — so reducing your impact feels achievable, not overwhelming.

FAQ

Common Questions

Our emission factors are based on internationally recognized data sources. Car travel uses 0.12 kg CO₂/km, electricity uses Pakistan's grid average of 0.4 kg CO₂/kWh, and meal calculations account for diet type (vegan, vegetarian, mixed, or meat-heavy). While these are averages and individual results may vary slightly, they provide a reliable baseline for understanding your impact.

Yes — completely. All your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your calculations, history, and profile settings never leave your device. This also means your data is specific to the browser you use.

After you calculate your footprint, GreenWallet sends your anonymous data (your kg values per category, diet type, and comparison to Pakistan average) to Anthropic's Claude API. Claude generates a personalized action plan referencing your actual numbers — not generic advice. The API call is made directly from your browser.

A mature tree absorbs approximately 22 kg of CO₂ per year. We multiply your daily footprint by 365 to get your annual CO₂, then divide by 22 to show how many trees would be needed to offset it. It's a relatable way to understand the scale of your impact.
